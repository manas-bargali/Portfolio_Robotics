Robotics Training for Children

As a Robotics Trainer, I conduct practical and fun hands-on sessions for school children.

  • Introduction to Microcontrollers (Arduino Uno)
  • Basic Electronics and Circuit Building on Breadboard
  • Working with LEDs, Buttons, Buzzers and Sensors
  • Controlling Motors and Servo Motors
  • Hands-on small projects like LED Patterns, Mood Light, Piano & LED Matrix
  • Developing logical thinking and problem-solving skills

My goal is to make robotics simple, exciting and accessible for every child.
